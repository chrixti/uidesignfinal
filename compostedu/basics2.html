What to Compost

Many organic materials can be composeted, including the following:

1. Fruit and vegetable peels and scraps

2. Rotten fruits and veggies

3. Houseplant trimmings

4. Coffee grounds and paper filters

5. Tea leaves

6. Eggshells

7. Nutshells (except walnuts)

8. Paper and cardboard
