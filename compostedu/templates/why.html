Why Compost at Home?

It's Easy!
  • It's a straightforward process that doesn't require much effort, specialized tools, or extensive knowledge.
Improve Your Soil!
  • Enriches soil and reduces the need for chemical fertilizers.
  • Improves soil health, suppresses diseases and pests, and boosts plant growth.
Help the Environment!
  • Reduces waste sent to landfills or incinerators
  • Minimizes the emission of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.
