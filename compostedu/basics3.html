What NOT to Compost

Some items are harmful to compost, as they may attract pests and rodents, contain bacteria, or be harmful to other plants. The following are items you should not compost:

1. Pet waste

2. Bones or scraps from meat

3. Dairy products

4. Leaves or twigs from black walnut trees

5. Walnuts

6. Coal ash or charcoal

7. Large pieces of wood

8. Fat, cooking oil, and grease
